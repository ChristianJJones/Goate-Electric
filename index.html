    Zeropoint

    Zeropoint is a digital energy that can wirelessly charge digital electronics and a digital asset that can be used as an asset in different places. Zeropoint is a stablecoin pegged to $0.10 per $ZPE. And wherever you go you can always access your phone, even if the battery already went to sleep.

    ZeropointWifi

    ZeropointWifi is a digital utility and digital asset, just like Zeropoint; except each $ZPW is a stablecoin pegged to $5 equalling a monthly subscription of unlimited wifi.
